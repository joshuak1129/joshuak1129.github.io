Health and Wellness with Kaleyeah

From the classroom to a healthier you

Hi there! My name is Josh Kim and I'm here to share some health/wellness resources with you. During the Spring 2018 semester, our project group, Kaleyeah, compiled a variety of resources covering topics ranging from stress management to tea's health benefits. Even better, we've employed a variety of mediums (newsletter, podcast, video) to get our message of health awareness out to you. Look below and see what interests you. Enjoy!

Drink Tea and Feel Tea-riffic

In this newsletter, I recommend three teas brimming with health benefits. If you want to feel healthier but think you're too busy to make any changes, this newsletter will make you rethink that. Start with something as quick and simple as brewing tea.

De-stress with Kaleyeah

Dan's Bandanna Project at Virginia Tech

In this video, we tackle the topic of mental health. In a series of interviews, brave students share personal stories to shed light on mental health issues. Furthermore, we discuss Dan's Bandanna Project at Virginia tech, an initiative to let Virginia Tech students know that they are never alone on their mental health journey.
