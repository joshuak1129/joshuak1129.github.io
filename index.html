ENGL 3844: Health and Wellness

From the classroom to you...

Hi there! My name is Josh Kim and I'm here to share some health/wellness resources with you. What started as a class project has developed into a full-fledged website for those who want to improve their health. In the past few months, our project group, Kaleyeah, has created a variety of resources covering various topics like stress management and tea's health benefits. Even better, we've employed a variety of mediums (newsletter, podcast, video) to get our message of health awareness out to you. Look below to see what interests you. Hope you enjoy!
